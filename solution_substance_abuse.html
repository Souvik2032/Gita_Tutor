Gita's Wisdom

In the Gita, Lord Krishna teaches the value of self-discipline and focus. For example, in Chapter 6, Verse 5 (Page 123) it is said:

उद्धरेदात्मनाऽत्मानं नाऽत्मानमवसादयेत्

Choose Your Language:

English: One must lift oneself up, not let oneself fall.

हिंदी: स्वयं को उठाओ, खुद को गिरने मत दो।

Real Life Example

Imagine a student who finds it hard to focus on studies because of many distractions. Just like a lamp that needs a steady wick to shine, the student needs to control distractions and work steadily. Following the Gita’s advice, by lifting oneself up (using simple daily habits and focus), the student can get back on track.
